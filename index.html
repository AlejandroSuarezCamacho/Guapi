The Pearl of the Pacific

Guapi is the gateway to the legendary Gorgona Island National Park, a paradise for ecotourism, scuba diving, and whale watching. Within the town, visitors can enjoy colorful streets filled with the rhythm of marimba music, taste exquisite Pacific cuisine rich in coconut, seafood, and ancestral recipes, and witness vibrant community traditions that have been passed down through generations.
